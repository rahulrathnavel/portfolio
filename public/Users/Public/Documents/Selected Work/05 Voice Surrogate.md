# Voice Surrogate — an accessibility conversation tool

Voice Surrogate is an Android accessibility prototype for non-speaking individuals. Its interaction centres on bilingual, on-device smart replies and text-to-speech.

## Rahul's role

Rahul built the Android prototype and its bilingual, on-device interaction flow.

## Product concerns

- accessibility before spectacle
- local/offline-friendly interaction
- bilingual smart replies
- clear text-to-speech feedback
