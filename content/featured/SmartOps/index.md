An AI-assisted incident-resolution prototype for cloud infrastructure. SmartOps brings telemetry, logs, diagnosis, suggested remediation, and human SRE review into one clear operating loop. Rahul led the AI/backend work, model and pipeline development, training, deployment, and proof-of-concept flow. The work reached the LTTS TECHgium 2026 national finalist stage as one of 34 teams from 62,000+ registrations.
